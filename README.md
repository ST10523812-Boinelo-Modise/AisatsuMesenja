The name of my app is called "AISATSU MESENJA".
"AISATSU" is the japanese word for "greeting" and "MESENJA" means "messenger".
The purpose of AISATSU MESENJA is for people who are constantly busy/occupied, which ends up deterioating their social life. This app helps 
them suggest or create social sparks to connect with friends and family.Each suggestion is different based on the time of day

I made sure that the app is attention grabbing for the user and has alot of fun suggestions by using japan's anime lifestyle
